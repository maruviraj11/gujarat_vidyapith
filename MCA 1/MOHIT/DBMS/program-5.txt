6. show supplier number and quantity for the part(s) with the highest weight.
>> select Sp.sno,Sp.qty 
	from sp Sp,p P 
		where P.weight=(select max(weight) from p) 
			and Sp.pno=P.pno;
			
			
	sno   qty
	
	S02	  232

7. Show supplier name and quantity for parts with color "Red".
>> select S.sname,Sp.qty,P.color 
	from s S,sp Sp,p P 
		where P.color='Red' 
			and Sp.sno=S.sno 
				and Sp.pno=P.pno;
				
				
	sname   qty   color
	
	Ashish  180   Red

8. Display supplier name,part city,and quantity where the supplier's status is less than 30 (from the shipment).
>> select S.sname,S.s_status,P.p_city,Sp.qty 
	from s S,sp Sp,p P 
		where S.s_status<30 
			and Sp.sno=S.sno 
				and Sp.pno=P.pno;

9. Display total quantity supplied by each supplier.
>> select S.sname,sum(Sp.qty)"Total Quantity" 
	from sp Sp,s S 
		where Sp.sno=S.sno 
			group by S.sno;
			
			
	sname      Total Quantity
	
	Abhimanyu             242
	Aakash                232
	Saurav                243
	Kushagra              130
	Ashish                180
